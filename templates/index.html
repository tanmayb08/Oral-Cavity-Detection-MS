What it accually do ?

The Oral Cavity Detection System website is a user-centric platform designed to provide a seamless and accessible experience for individuals seeking to assess their oral health. Leveraging advanced image processing techniques and TensorFlow's object detection API, this innovative system offers a non-invasive means for early detection of cavities through the convenience of a web-based interface.
